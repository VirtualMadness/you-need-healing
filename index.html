T.R.I.S

You, as T.R.I.S., will have to travel through planets, gathering water drops to buy your partners' freedom. Be wary of enemies, traps, screen borders... ahem.Be wary, that's all!

Controls:

  • LEFT & RIGHT to move.
  • UP/W & DOWN/S to change gravity direction.
  • R to return to the last checkpoint.
  • T to travel to the start of the level.

Made with GameMaker Studio for GBJAM 5

Play it!

Super Bad Heroes

Times have changed. That accident permuted your powers and made them a national security problem. That's not totally true. But they're shittier Of course, your former fans are now enraged killing machines that want to destroy your statues. Stop them or something. It's up to you.

Controls:

  • WASD // Left Stick - Move
  • Cursor // Right Stick - Aim
  • LMB // Right trigger - Primary fire
  • RMB // Left trigger - Secondary fire (Only Magna).

Made with GameMaker Studio for Student GameJam

Play it!
